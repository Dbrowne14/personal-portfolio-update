# Portfolio Content Brief

## Core positioning line

From evaluating products to shipping them.

## Supporting statement

I spent eight years evaluating technology companies and working alongside founders and product teams. Now I design, build and ship the products myself.

This can be refined editorially, but the meaning should remain intact.

Show six projects in this order:

## Tone

The whole site should feel:

- Professional
- Confident
- Approachable
- Thoughtful
- Curious
- Editorial
- Technically credible

Avoid language that assumes every visitor is a freelance client.

Avoid:

- Let's build something together
- Your next project
- Bring your vision to life
- Ready to get started?
- Next milestone
- Agency-style sales claims

Contact language should welcome:

- Full-time engineering opportunities
- Recruiter conversations
- Product and technical discussions
- Collaboration
- Networking
- Occasional suitable freelance work

Freelance work is possible but not the primary positioning.
